The Moonwalker

The Moonwalker is an autonomous robot created using Java and the Lego Mindstorm kit in a team of 5. The main tasks for the robot were to localize itself and navigate to a specific area on a 12’ x 12’ enclosure containing various obstacles. Once it was in the specified area, the robot was supposed to shoot ping pong balls on specific targets. My contributions to this project were Managing and implementing localization and odometry correction algorithms.

SONO

SONO is an iOS application that collects, displays and uploads physiological signals from empatica's E4 wristband to a server in the goal of using this data to classify emotions through machine learning. The physiological signals are also mapped into music (biomusic) in real time. The goal of this application is to make the task of understanding and interacting with non-communicative inidividuals easier, by expressing their emotions in the form of music

Drive Safe

Drive safe is an android application that we built in a team of 4 during WearHacks, a wearables hackathon held in Montreal in 2015. The app monitors the brain activity of truck drivers through EEG signals provided by the Muse Headband, and alerts them in times of loss of concentration by vibrating their pebble watch to keep the roads safer. The data collected is stored in a database to build a complete profile of each driver.
Awards: Best Application of Analytics Award in Wearhacks and top 10 hacks

Artemis

A Mars Rover designed and built by McGill Robotics to compete in the University Rover Challenge in Utah, and the European Rover Challenge in Poland. Artemis was remotely controlled to complete certain tasks such as terrain traversal, astronaut assistance, panel servicing as well as a science task that involves obtaining and analyzing soil samples. My contributions to this project were designing PCB schematics, soldering components onto the boards as well as establishing the RS485 communications protocol using C and Arduino. The protocol governed communication over the drive system, arm controllers, end effectors, servo controllers and battery controllers.
Awards: 3rd place European Rover Challenge (Summer 2015)
